---
title: "Document needed tweaks"
author: "Ben Orenstein"
chapter: "after-your-talk"
order: 3
---

Shortly after you finish your talk, jot down a few notes about how it could be improved.

There are things you'll learn about your talk after giving it live that are near-impossible to know or anticipate. Capture
those valuable bits of knowledge so you can tweak your talk before your next performance of it.
